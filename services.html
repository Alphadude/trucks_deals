Our Services

What We Do?

Container Haulage

We specialize in the secure and timely transportation of containers across Nigeria. Whether it's raw materials, finished products, or machinery, our fleet of well-maintained trucks ensures that your containers are delivered safely to their destination Whether it’s 2x20ft, 1x40ft, or 1x20ft containers, we ensure safe and timely delivery of your cargo.

Our Container Haulage Services include:

  • Nationwide container transportation.
  • Fleet Flexibility: Equipped for various container sizes
  • GPS tracking for real-time updates on your cargo.
  • Professional drivers and safety-compliant trucks
  • Flexible schedules to meet your business needs.
Heavy-Duty Equipment Transport

Moving large, heavy-duty equipment requires precision and expertise. Our experienced team specializes in transporting oversized or high-value industrial equipment to various locations across Nigeria, ensuring safe delivery, no matter the distance.

Our Heavy-Duty Transport Services include

  • Safe handling of industrial equipment and heavy machinery.
  • Customized transport solutions based on your specific requirements.
  • Use of specialized trucks for oversized or heavy items.
  • Experienced operators trained in secure loading and unloading techniques.
Car Delivery

We offer secure and efficient car delivery services for individuals and businesses. Whether you’re transporting a single vehicle or an entire fleet, we ensure your cars are delivered in pristine condition, with full transparency throughout the journey.

Our Car Delivery Services include

  • Nationwide car transport for individuals and dealerships.
  • Enclosed and open car carriers, depending on your preference.
  • Vehicle condition reports before and after transport.
  • Secure loading, transportation, and unloading

House Moving

Relocating your home or office? We provide comprehensive house moving services, handling every step of the process with care and professionalism. From packing to delivery, our goal is to make your move as stress-free as possible.

Our House Moving Services include

  • Packing, loading, and secure transportation of your belongings.
  • Customized moving solutions for residential and corporate clients.
  • Expert handling of fragile items and valuables.
  • Timely delivery and unpacking services at your new location.
  • Uninstallations and re-installations
Fleet Management

At Trucks & Deal Logistics, we offer efficient fleet management services to help businesses optimize their logistics operations. Whether you own a small fleet or a large one, we can help streamline operations, reduce costs, and improve performance.

Our Fleet Management Services include

  • GPS tracking and fleet monitoring for real-time updates
  • Maintenance scheduling and fuel management.
  • Driver management and performance tracking.
  • Compliance with safety and regulatory requirements.
  • Trip scheduling
Custom Clearing & Forwarding

Navigating customs processes can be complex, but our team of experts is here to simplify the process. We provide seamless customs clearance and forwarding services, ensuring your goods move smoothly through the entire supply chain.

Our Custom Clearing & Forwarding Services include

  • Import and export customs clearance.
  • Handling of all documentation and compliance processes
  • End-to-end logistics, from port to final destination.
  • Dedicated support to navigate Nigerian customs regulations.
Oil & Gas Logistics

Located in the heart of Nigeria’s oil and gas region, we understand the complexities of transporting oil and gas materials. Our specialized oil and gas logistics services are designed to meet the stringent safety and operational requirements of this sector.

Our Oil & Gas Logistics Services include:

  • Transportation of oil and gas materials and equipment.
  • Compliance with industry safety standards.
  • Experienced team familiar with the needs of the sector.
  • Timely deliveries to oil and gas fields and refineries.
