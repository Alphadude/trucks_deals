Our History

Trucks & Deal Logistics Limited was established with a clear vision to revolutionize the logistics industry in Nigeria. Over the years, we have grown from a small operation in Port Harcourt to a nationwide service provider. Our fleet of CNG-powered trucks and experienced team have enabled us to maintain a reputation for reliability, even on the most challenging routes. Today, we continue to expand, offering cutting-edge logistics solutions for businesses and individuals alike.
