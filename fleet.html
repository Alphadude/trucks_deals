Fleet

Our Fleet

At Trucks & Deal Logistics Limited, we take pride in our diverse and well-maintained fleet of vehicles, designed to meet the unique transportation needs of our clients across Nigeria. Our commitment to safety, efficiency, and reliability ensures that your cargo is transported securely and on time. Explore our fleet options below:.


Flatbed Trailers

Capacity: 28-60 tons

Our flatbed trailers are ideal for transporting containers and oil and gas tools. They offer a stable and secure platform for heavy loads, making them perfect for projects that require flexibility and efficiency.

Key Features:

  • Open design allows for easy loading and unloading.
  • Suitable for containers and pipesite.
  • Equipped with safety straps and tarps for secure transport.
Lowbed Trailers

Capacity: 40-120 tons

Our lowbed trailers are specially designed for heavy-duty equipment transport. With a lower deck height, they provide stability and safety for oversized loads, ensuring that your equipment arrives at its destination without any damage.

Key Features:

  • Suitable for oversized and heavy items.
  • Ideal for transporting construction machinery and industrial equipment.
  • Equipped with ramps for easy loading and unloading.
  • Enhanced stability for heavy loads during transit.
Sided/Open Body Trucks

Capacity: 30-60tons

These trucks are perfect for transporting food grains and manufacturing chemicals. Their open body design allows for easy access and loading, while the trampoline feature ensures that your cargo remains secure during transit.

Key Features:

  • Flexible loading options for various types of cargo.
  • Ideal for bulk transportation.
  • Compliance with safety standards for transporting food and chemicals.

Box Body Trailers and Short Trucks

Capacity:5-35 tons Varies

Our box body trailers and short trucks are versatile solutions for relocation, fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG), grains, and other general cargo. They provide secure storage and protection from external elements during transport.

Key Features:

  • Lockable doors for added security.
  • Ideal for transporting goods that require protection from weather conditions.
  • Available in various sizes to meet your needs.
Modular Trucks

Capacity: Varies for oversized and overweight loads

Our modular trucks are designed specifically for transporting oversized and overweight loads. They are equipped with advanced technology to ensure safe and compliant transport for large cargo.

Key Features:

  • Customizable configurations for different load sizes.
  • Equipped with specialized features for safe handling of oversized loads.
  • Adherence to all regulatory requirements for oversized transport.
